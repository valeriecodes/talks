Adventures in localization and internationalization

Defining our terms

  • Localization - the process of adapting internationalized software for a specific region or language by adding locale-specific components and translating text
  • Internationalization- the process of designing a software application so that it can potentially be adapted to various languages and regions without engineering changes

Thanks, Wikipedia!

What we talk about when we talk about translation.

  • Language-English to French, French to Arabic, English to German, Swahili to Esperanto, Spanish to Cantonese
  • Cultural-British to American, Portuguese to Brazilian, Canadian to Australian
  • Register-Formal to informal, audience differences
